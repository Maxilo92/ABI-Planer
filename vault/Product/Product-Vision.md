# Product Vision

ABI Planer is a planning platform for school and cohort coordination. The product should help people manage planning work with low friction, clear status, and strong guardrails.

## What the product should feel like

- focused rather than crowded
- structured rather than decorative
- calm rather than noisy
- trustworthy rather than flashy

## Core domains

- finances
- calendar and events
- todos and assignments
- news and communication
- roles and approvals
- cloud-backed collaboration

## Non-goals for the handbook

- do not treat the vault as a second product spec copy
- do not re-declare canonical security rules if the source file already exists
- do not hide tradeoffs; name them clearly
