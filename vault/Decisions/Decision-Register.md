# Decision Register

Use this note to record important choices that should survive beyond one task.

## Entry format

- decision
- context
- alternatives
- tradeoff
- consequence
- canonical sources

## Good candidates

- why a flow stays server-side
- why a route belongs on a specific host
- why a feature needs a dedicated note or system deep dive
- why a rule, schema field, or release step exists
